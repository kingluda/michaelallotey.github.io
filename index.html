// what I work on

Research Focus

🌡️
Urban Heat & Land Surface Temperature
Examining the relationship between LST and landscape spectral indices in semi-arid urban environments. Led to a peer-reviewed publication in Local Environment (2025) on Tamale's thermal landscape.
🌳
LiDAR & Tree Canopy Analysis
Fusing LiDAR point cloud data with multispectral aerial imagery (NAIP) to quantify urban tree canopy extent and density — critical inputs for urban climate resilience and heat mitigation planning.
🌊
Surface Water Change Detection
Time-series analysis of global surface water dynamics using the JRC dataset in Google Earth Engine. Built a publicly deployed interactive web application enabling anyone to explore hydrological change at planetary scale.
🤖
Machine Learning for Spatial Analytics
Applying Random Forest, deep learning, and computer vision to geospatial prediction problems — from nonprofit donor targeting to farmland boundary delineation across all of Africa.
🗺️
Geospatial Data Infrastructure
Designing and deploying spatial data infrastructure for national-scale programs — including the National WASH Data Infrastructure Framework and electoral monitoring systems for Ghana's Electoral Commission.
🌾
Agricultural & Food Systems Mapping
Mapping active farmland boundaries across Africa at continental scale. Published research on food insecurity, school readiness, and land use sustainability across Sub-Saharan Africa.
